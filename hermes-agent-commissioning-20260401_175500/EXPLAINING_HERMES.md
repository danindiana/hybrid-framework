# Your New Digital Chief of Staff: Hermes Agent ⚕

This document explains what we have just built and "commissioned" on this system in plain language.

## 1. An AI with "Hands"
Unlike standard AI (like ChatGPT) that can only talk or give advice, Hermes is **physically integrated** into this computer. It doesn't just tell you how to change a security setting; it can actually reach out, back up your files, change the system configuration, and restart services itself. It has "hands" to perform technical labor.

## 2. Total Situational Awareness
Through the "commissioning" process, Hermes now understands exactly what tools it has at its disposal. It knows it is sitting on a high-performance machine with:
*   **The Muscle:** A massive 32-core processor and two powerful NVIDIA graphics cards.
*   **The Memory:** 128GB of RAM and over 10 terabytes of storage.
*   **The Skills:** 74 pre-installed "skill modules" ranging from managing your code to organizing documents and controlling smart home devices.

## 3. A Resident that Remembers
Most AIs forget who you are the moment you close the chat. Hermes is a "resident" of this system. It lives in its own dedicated workspace and builds a "user model" of you over time. It learns your preferences, your specific hardware quirks, and how you like things organized, getting smarter the more you use it.

## 4. Your System's New Translator
Hermes acts as a bridge between high-level human goals ("Make my system more secure") and the complex, messy world of technical commands. We have already put it to work by:
*   **Securing the "Front Door":** Moving the SSH port to a non-standard location to stop automated hackers.
*   **Updating the System Notice:** It has already left a permanent mark on the system's "Message of the Day" so any user logging in knows the new security rules.
