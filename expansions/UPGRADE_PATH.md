# Upgrade Path Speculation

## Stage 1: The Tool-Assisted Phase (Current)
- AI is a tool; Human is the master.
- Focus: Speed and Precision.

## Stage 2: The Co-Processor Phase (Short-term)
- AI and Human work in parallel. 
- AI starts suggesting Axiom Shifts autonomously.
- Focus: Consensus and Alignment.

## Stage 3: The Integrated Hive (Mid-term)
- Multiple AI agents manage sub-systems; Human manages the "Global Intent."
- Semantic Drift is automatically corrected without Human intervention.
- Focus: Autonomy and Self-Healing.

## Stage 4: The Transfinite Singularity (Long-term)
- The boundary between Human intent and Machine execution dissolves.
- Code is generated, verified, and refactored in a continuous stream of pure semantic logic.
- Focus: Infinite Complexity Management.
